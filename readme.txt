Title: Bonsai Haven
Created by: Emma Waugh (2023001068)

Description:
Bonsai Haven is a website designed to serve as a comprehensive platform for bonsai enthusiasts. 
It offers a variety of bonsai plants for purchase and provides detailed information on their care. 
The website aims to create a serene and visually appealing experience with a focus on green tones 
that reflect the natural beauty of bonsai. Users can navigate through different sections such as 
Plants/Shop, Plant Care, Contact Us, and About Us. The site structure ensures a user-friendly shopping 
and educational experience, encouraging users to explore and engage with the bonsai community.
